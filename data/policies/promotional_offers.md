## 1. Overview

This policy governs all promotional offers, discount codes, coupons, flash sales, and special pricing events on the The Platform platform. All support agents must reference this policy when handling promo-related inquiries.

## 2. Types of Promotions

### 2.1 Discount Coupons (Promo Codes)
- Alphanumeric codes entered at checkout (e.g., SAVE20, WELCOME10).
- Types:
  - **Percentage discount:** e.g., 20% off (capped at a maximum discount amount)
  - **Fixed amount discount:** e.g., $15 off orders over $75
  - **Free shipping:** Overrides standard shipping charges
  - **BOGO (Buy One, Get One):** Free or discounted item on qualifying purchases

### 2.2 Automatic Discounts
- Applied automatically at checkout without needing a code
- Displayed on product pages and in the cart
- Examples: "Buy 3, Get 10% Off," seasonal auto-discounts, bulk pricing

### 2.3 Flash Sales
- Limited-time sales events lasting **2-24 hours**
- Products are discounted by **20-70%** during the event
- Quantities are limited — once stock runs out, the sale price expires
- No rainchecks: If an item sells out during a flash sale, The Platform does not honor the sale price for future restocking

### 2.4 Loyalty Member Exclusive Offers
- Available only to members of the The Platform Loyalty Program
- Typically **5-15%** additional discount on select categories
- Loyalty offers can be combined with certain promo codes (see stacking rules in Section 4)

### 2.5 Referral Discounts
- **Referrer:** Receives a **$10 credit** when the referred person makes their first purchase of $50+
- **Referred customer:** Receives **15% off** their first order (max $25 discount)
- Referral credits expire **90 days** after being issued
- Maximum **10 referral credits** per account per calendar year
- Referral benefits do not apply to gift card purchases or subscription orders

### 2.6 Birthday Discounts
- Loyalty members receive a **20% off** coupon valid for 7 days around their birthday (3 days before through 4 days after)
- Maximum discount: $50
- Valid on a single order; non-transferable
- Cannot be combined with other percentage-based coupons

## 3. Coupon Rules and Restrictions

### 3.1 General Rules
- Only **one promo code** can be applied per order (unless specifically stated as stackable).
- Promo codes are case-insensitive (e.g., SAVE20 = save20).
- Minimum purchase requirements must be met after other discounts and before tax/shipping.
- Promo codes cannot be applied retroactively to orders that have already been placed.
- Promo codes cannot be used on: gift cards, digital subscriptions, marketplace seller items (unless the seller participates), or items already marked as "Final Sale."

### 3.2 Expiration
- All promo codes have an expiration date displayed in the promotional email/advertisement.
- Expired promo codes cannot be honored. Support agents are not authorized to extend or reactivate expired promo codes.
- Exception: If a promo code fails due to a **technical glitch** during its valid period (documented by the engineering team), a one-time manual discount may be applied by a support supervisor.

### 3.3 Geographic Restrictions
- Some promo codes are region-specific (e.g., US-only, EU-only).
- The geographic restriction is based on the **shipping address**, not the billing address or account location.
- Region-specific promos are clearly labeled in the promotional material.

### 3.4 Single-Use vs. Multi-Use
- **Single-use codes:** Tied to a specific customer account. Cannot be shared or reused. (e.g., JANE-WELCOME-15)
- **Multi-use codes:** Can be used by any customer, subject to an overall redemption limit. (e.g., SUMMER25 — valid for the first 5,000 redemptions)
- Abuse of multi-use codes (e.g., creating multiple accounts) is a violation of The Platform Terms of Service and may result in order cancellation and account suspension.

## 4. Stacking Rules

### 4.1 Allowed Combinations
| Promo Type | + Promo Code | + Auto Discount | + Loyalty Discount | + Free Ship Promo |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Promo Code** | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Auto Discount** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Loyalty Discount** | ❌ | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ |
| **Free Ship Promo** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ |

**Summary:**
- You **CANNOT** combine two promo codes.
- You **CANNOT** combine a promo code with a loyalty-exclusive discount.
- Automatic discounts stack with everything except another automatic discount on the same item.
- Free shipping promos stack with most other offers.

### 4.2 Discount Order of Application
When multiple valid discounts apply, they are applied in this order:
1. Automatic discounts (product-level)
2. Promo code discount (order-level)
3. Loyalty tier discount (if no promo code is applied)
4. Store credit / gift card balance
5. Free shipping (if applicable)

## 5. Price Matching

### 5.1 The Platform Price Match Guarantee
- The Platform will match a lower price from a **qualifying competitor** if the customer reports it within **7 days** of purchase.
- **Qualifying competitors:** Amazon.com, Walmart.com, Target.com, BestBuy.com (US only)
- The item must be identical (same model, brand, SKU, condition — new only).
- The competitor's price must be their current, publicly listed price (not a clearance, outlet, or membership-exclusive price).
- The price match is applied as a refund of the difference to the original payment method.

### 5.2 Exclusions from Price Matching
- Marketplace/third-party seller items (on either The Platform or the competitor)
- Items purchased during flash sales or with promo codes
- Bundle deals or combo offers
- Items with a purchase limit (e.g., "limit 2 per customer")
- Prices that appear to be the result of a technical error
- International prices (price matching is US domestic only)

## 6. Handling Coupon-Related Complaints

### 6.1 "Coupon Didn't Work at Checkout"
1. Verify the code is valid (not expired, correct spelling).
2. Check product eligibility (not a restricted category).
3. Check minimum order value.
4. Check geographic restriction.
5. If all conditions are met and the code still fails, escalate to the Promotions Team with the promo code, order number, and screenshots.

### 6.2 "I Forgot to Apply My Coupon"
- Promo codes **cannot** be applied after order placement.
- If the order has not yet shipped, the customer may cancel and re-order with the coupon applied.
- Support agents are **not authorized** to apply retroactive discounts or issue manual credits in lieu of a coupon, unless approved by a supervisor.

### 6.3 "The Sale Price Changed Before I Checked Out"
- Flash sale and promotional prices are valid only while stock lasts and during the stated promotion period.
- If a customer had the item in their cart but the price changed before checkout, The Platform is not obligated to honor the previous price.
- As a goodwill gesture, a supervisor may approve a **5% courtesy discount** (max $20) for affected customers.
