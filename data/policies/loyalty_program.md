## 1. Overview

The The Platform Loyalty Program rewards repeat customers with exclusive discounts, perks, and priority services across all tiers. This policy defines the program structure, earning and redemption rules, and tier benefits.

## 2. Program Tiers

### 2.1 Tier Structure

| Tier | Annual Spend Requirement | Points Multiplier | Badge |
|------|--------------------------|-------------------|-------|
| **Bronze** | $0 (auto-enrolled) | 1x | 🥉 |
| **Silver** | $500+ | 1.5x | 🥈 |
| **Gold** | $1,500+ | 2x | 🥇 |
| **Platinum** | $5,000+ | 3x | 💎 |

### 2.2 Tier Qualification
- Tier qualification is based on **cumulative spend** within a **rolling 12-month period** (calendar months, not from enrollment date).
- Only eligible purchases count toward tier qualification. Excluded: gift cards, taxes, shipping charges, and items that were returned.
- Tier status is evaluated monthly. If spend falls below the qualification threshold at the end of the 12-month rolling period, the customer is demoted one tier (with a **30-day grace period** before benefits change).
- Tier promotions (upgrades) are applied **immediately** upon reaching a spend threshold.

## 3. Points System

### 3.1 Earning Points
- **Base rate:** 1 point per $1 spent (before tax, after discounts)
- Points multipliers apply based on tier (see table above)
- **Bonus points events:** The Platform runs 2x/3x/5x bonus point events throughout the year, announced via email
- Points are credited after the return window closes (30 days from delivery) to prevent earn-and-return abuse

### 3.2 Redeeming Points
- Points can be redeemed for **The Platform Wallet credit** at a rate of **100 points = $1.00**
- Minimum redemption: **500 points** ($5.00)
- Points can be redeemed at checkout or through the Loyalty dashboard
- Points cannot be redeemed for cash, transferred to another account, or used to purchase gift cards

### 3.3 Points Expiration
- Points expire **18 months** after being earned if no purchase or redemption activity occurs on the account
- At least one qualifying purchase or redemption within any 18-month window resets the expiration clock for ALL points
- Expired points are non-recoverable
- Customers receive email reminders **60 days** and **30 days** before points are set to expire

## 4. Tier Benefits

### 4.1 Bronze Tier (Default)
- Access to the The Platform Loyalty points system
- Birthday discount: 10% off (max $20), valid 7 days
- Early access to annual sale events (2 hours before general public)

### 4.2 Silver Tier ($500+ annual spend)
All Bronze benefits plus:
- **Free standard shipping** on all orders (no minimum)
- Birthday discount: 15% off (max $30), valid 7 days
- Exclusive Silver-only promotional events (2-3 per year)
- Priority email support (48-hour response guarantee)

### 4.3 Gold Tier ($1,500+ annual spend)
All Silver benefits plus:
- **Free expedited shipping** on orders over $75
- Birthday discount: 20% off (max $50), valid 7 days
- **Extended return window:** 45 days (vs. standard 30 days)
- **5% loyalty discount** on select categories (home, fashion, electronics) — auto-applied at checkout
- Dedicated support queue (4-hour response guarantee)
- Early access to new product launches (24 hours before general availability)

### 4.4 Platinum Tier ($5,000+ annual spend)
All Gold benefits plus:
- **Free standard, expedited, overnight shipping** on all orders + **free same-day delivery** in eligible metro areas
- Birthday discount: 25% off (max $75), valid 14 days
- **Extended return window:** 60 days
- **10% loyalty discount** on all categories — auto-applied at checkout
- **Priority customer support:** Dedicated Platinum phone line with <2 minute wait time
- **Concierge service:** Personal shopping assistant for product recommendations and VIP access to limited-edition products
- **$20 inconvenience credit** for The Platform-initiated cancellations (vs. $10 for other tiers)
- Annual **$50 loyalty bonus credit** (credited to The Platform Wallet on the account anniversary)
- Free gift wrapping on all orders
- Exclusive invitations to The Platform partner events and brand previews

## 5. Loyalty Program Restrictions

### 5.1 Account Limitations
- Each customer is limited to **one loyalty account** per person
- Loyalty accounts are tied to the customer's verified email address and cannot be merged after creation
- Points and tier status are non-transferable between accounts
- Households sharing an address may have separate loyalty accounts, but The Platform may review unusual earning patterns

### 5.2 Abuse Prevention
- Purchasing items solely for the purpose of earning points and subsequently returning them ("churning") is prohibited
- Accounts exhibiting churning behavior may have points revoked and tier status frozen
- Using multiple accounts to circumvent referral limits or accumulate benefits is a violation of Terms of Service

## 6. Program Changes

- The Platform reserves the right to modify tier thresholds, benefits, and point values with **60 days' notice** to members
- Material changes are communicated via email and in-app notification
- Points earned before a program change retain their value through the transition period (minimum 90 days)
