## 1. Overview

This policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancelling orders on the The Platform platform. Cancellation eligibility depends on the order's fulfillment stage at the time of the cancellation request.

## 2. Cancellation Windows

### 2.1 Before Processing (Full Cancellation)
- Orders can be cancelled for a **full refund** if the cancellation request is made **before the order enters the "Processing" stage**.
- Typically, orders remain in "Confirmed" status for **1-2 hours** after placement during standard business hours.
- Cancellation at this stage is instant and requires no further action from the customer.

### 2.2 During Processing (Attempt to Cancel)
- Once an order is in "Processing" (picked, but not yet shipped), cancellation is **attempted but not guaranteed**.
- The Platform will contact the fulfillment center to attempt cancellation. This process takes up to **4 hours**.
- If cancellation is successful, a full refund is issued.
- If the item has already been packed/labeled, it will ship, and the customer must return it per the Returns & Refunds Policy.

### 2.3 After Shipping (Cannot Cancel)
- Orders that have been shipped **cannot be cancelled**.
- The customer must wait to receive the package and then initiate a return.
- Exception: If the carrier has not yet picked up the package (status shows "Label Created" for more than 24 hours), The Platform may intercept the shipment. In this case, a full refund is issued upon confirmation.

## 3. Multi-Item Order Cancellation

### 3.1 Partial Cancellation
- Customers may cancel individual items in a multi-item order without affecting the remaining items.
- The refund applies only to the cancelled item(s).
- If the partial cancellation brings the order total below the free shipping threshold, the original shipping cost is **not retroactively charged** to the customer.

### 3.2 Bundle/Set Cancellation
- Items purchased as a bundle or set must be cancelled as a complete unit. Individual items within a bundle cannot be cancelled separately.
- If a bundle item is out of stock after order placement, The Platform cancels the entire bundle and issues a full refund.

## 4. Cancellation by The Platform

### 4.1 Reasons for Seller-Initiated Cancellation
The Platform may cancel an order for the following reasons:
- Item is out of stock or discontinued
- Pricing error (significantly below intended price due to technical glitch)
- Suspected fraudulent order (flagged by the Trust & Safety team)
- Customer's payment method declined after initial authorization
- Regulatory or legal restrictions (e.g., age-restricted items shipped to ineligible recipients)

### 4.2 Communication
- Customers are notified via email and in-app notification immediately upon seller-initiated cancellation.
- The notification includes the reason for cancellation and any recommended alternatives.
- A full refund is issued automatically within **24 hours** of The Platform-initiated cancellation.

### 4.3 Compensation for The Platform-Initiated Cancellations
- If The Platform cancels an order due to a stock or pricing issue:
  - The customer receives a **$10 inconvenience credit** (The Platform Wallet)
  - Platinum members receive a **$20 inconvenience credit**
  - If the cancellation occurs on a time-sensitive purchase (gift with a specific event date), additional compensation up to **$50** may be offered at the discretion of the support supervisor.

## 5. Pre-Order and Backorder Cancellations

### 5.1 Pre-Order Cancellation
- Pre-orders can be cancelled at any time before the item ships with a full refund.
- No cancellation fees apply to pre-orders.
- If the pre-order item's price changes between order placement and shipping, the customer is charged/refunded the difference and may cancel at that point.

### 5.2 Backorder Cancellation
- Items on backorder can be cancelled at any time before shipment.
- If the estimated backorder fulfillment date is extended by more than **14 days** from the original estimate, the customer is proactively notified and given the option to cancel or continue waiting.

## 6. Subscription Order Cancellations

### 6.1 Subscription Cancellation
- Subscription orders (auto-replenishment) can be cancelled at any time through the customer's account settings.
- If a subscription order has already been submitted for the current cycle, it follows the same cancellation rules as standard orders (see Section 2).
- Cancelling a subscription does not affect previously shipped orders.

### 6.2 Subscription Pause
- Customers may pause their subscription for up to **3 months** without cancellation.
- Pausing does not affect the customer's subscription pricing or discount tier.

## 7. Refund Processing for Cancellations

- **Credit/Debit Card:** Refund within **3-5 business days** of cancellation confirmation
- **The Platform Wallet:** Instant refund upon cancellation confirmation
- **PayPal:** Refund within **3-5 business days**
- **Cash on Delivery:** If the order was COD and the customer pre-paid any partial amount, refund via bank transfer within **5-7 business days**
- **Gift Card/Store Credit:** Balance restored within **24 hours**
