## 1. Overview

This policy governs all domestic shipping operations for The Platform within the United States (including territories). It covers shipping methods, delivery timelines, free shipping thresholds, and handling of delivery-related issues.

## 2. Shipping Methods and Delivery Timelines

### 2.1 Standard Shipping
- **Delivery Time:** 5-8 business days from order confirmation
- **Cost:** $4.99 per order (flat rate, regardless of weight up to 70 lbs)
- **Carrier:** USPS, UPS Ground, or FedEx Ground (assigned automatically based on destination and package dimensions)
- **Tracking:** Full tracking provided via The Platform order tracking page
- **Availability:** All 50 US states, DC, Puerto Rico, and US Virgin Islands

### 2.2 Expedited Shipping
- **Delivery Time:** 2-3 business days from order confirmation
- **Cost:** $9.99 per order (flat rate for items under 20 lbs), $14.99 for items 20-70 lbs
- **Carrier:** UPS 2-Day or FedEx 2-Day
- **Cutoff Time:** Orders placed before **2:00 PM EST** ship the same business day
- **Availability:** Continental US only (48 states + DC). Not available for Alaska, Hawaii, or US territories.

### 2.3 Overnight/Next-Day Shipping
- **Delivery Time:** 1 business day from order confirmation
- **Cost:** $19.99 per order (items under 10 lbs), $29.99 (items 10-50 lbs)
- **Carrier:** UPS Next Day Air or FedEx Priority Overnight
- **Cutoff Time:** Orders placed before **12:00 PM (Noon) EST** qualify for next-business-day delivery
- **Availability:** Continental US only. Not available for PO Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, or rural routes.
- **Restrictions:** Not available for oversized items (any dimension > 60 inches), hazardous materials, or items fulfilled by third-party sellers.

### 2.4 Same-Day Delivery
- **Delivery Time:** Within 6 hours of order confirmation
- **Cost:** $14.99 (items under 10 lbs), **FREE** for Platinum Tier members
- **Availability:** Select metro areas only: New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Houston, Phoenix, San Francisco, Seattle, Miami, Dallas, Atlanta, Boston, Denver
- **Cutoff Time:** Orders placed before **10:00 AM local time**
- **Restrictions:** Limited to items stocked in local The Platform fulfillment hubs. Not available for marketplace/third-party seller items.

## 3. Free Shipping

### 3.1 Free Shipping Threshold
- Orders totaling **$35 or more** qualify for **free standard shipping** (5-8 business days).
- The $35 threshold applies to the subtotal after discounts/coupons, before tax.
- Gift card purchases do not count toward the free shipping threshold.
- Items from multiple sellers can be combined to reach the threshold.

### 3.2 Free Shipping — Loyalty Program
- **Silver Tier:** Free standard shipping on all orders (no minimum)
- **Gold Tier:** Free standard shipping on all orders + free expedited shipping on orders over $75
- **Platinum Tier:** Free standard, expedited, and overnight shipping on all orders + free same-day delivery in eligible areas

### 3.3 Free Shipping Promotions
- The Platform periodically offers free shipping promotions (e.g., Free Shipping Week, Black Friday Free Shipping). These are announced via email and on the The Platform homepage.
- Promotional free shipping applies only during the promotion period and follows the promotion's specific terms.

## 4. Order Processing

### 4.1 Processing Times
- **Standard items (The Platform fulfilled):** 1-2 business days for processing before shipping
- **Marketplace seller items:** 1-3 business days, depending on the seller's processing time (displayed on the product page)
- **Made-to-order/custom items:** 5-10 business days for processing before shipping
- **Pre-order items:** Ship on the announced release date. Customers are notified if there are delays.

### 4.2 Business Days Definition
- Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays.
- Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed on the next business day.
- Federal holidays observed: New Year's Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Columbus Day, Veterans Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day.

### 4.3 Order Cutoff Times (Summary)

| Shipping Method | Cutoff Time (EST) | Delivery Speed |
|-----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Standard | End of business day | 5-8 business days |
| Expedited | 2:00 PM | 2-3 business days |
| Overnight | 12:00 PM (Noon) | 1 business day |
| Same-Day | 10:00 AM local | Within 6 hours |

## 5. Shipping Restrictions

### 5.1 Weight and Size Limits
- **Maximum package weight:** 150 lbs per individual package
- **Items > 70 lbs:** Ship via freight carrier (LTL). Additional $49.99 freight surcharge applies. Delivery time: 7-14 business days.
- **Oversized items (any dimension > 108 inches):** Must ship via freight. Customer contacted for delivery scheduling.

### 5.2 Address Restrictions
- **PO Boxes:** Standard shipping only. Expedited and overnight not available.
- **APO/FPO/DPO (Military):** Standard shipping only. Delivery time: 10-21 business days. Free shipping for active military personnel (verification required).
- **US Territories (Guam, USVI, American Samoa, Northern Mariana Islands):** Standard shipping only. Additional surcharge of $9.99 may apply. Delivery time: 10-15 business days.

### 5.3 Restricted Items
The following items have special shipping requirements:
- **Lithium batteries:** Ground shipping only (no air freight). Cannot ship to PO Boxes.
- **Flammable/hazardous materials:** Ground shipping only within continental US. Not available for Alaska, Hawaii, or territories.
- **Alcohol and tobacco:** Shipped only to states where permitted by law. Adult signature required (21+ ID verification at delivery).
- **Firearms and ammunition:** Shipped only to licensed FFL dealers. Additional regulatory compliance required.
- **Live animals/plants:** Expedited shipping only to minimize transit time. Not available during extreme weather periods.

## 6. Delivery Issues

### 6.1 Late Delivery
- If a package arrives later than the estimated delivery window:
  - **Standard shipping:** The Platform offers a **$5 credit** for orders arriving 3+ days beyond the estimated window.
  - **Expedited shipping:** Full shipping cost refunded if delivery exceeds 4 business days.
  - **Overnight shipping:** Full shipping cost refunded + **$10 credit** if delivery takes more than 2 business days.
  - **Same-Day delivery:** Full shipping cost refunded + **$15 credit** if delivery is not completed on the ordered day.

### 6.2 Lost Packages
- If tracking shows no update for **7 business days** after the last scan, the package is considered lost.
- The Platform will either reship the order or issue a full refund at the customer's preference.
- Lost package claims must be filed within **30 days** of the estimated delivery date.

### 6.3 Damaged in Transit
- If a package arrives with visible damage, the customer should:
  1. Photograph the damaged packaging and item(s) before discarding anything
  2. Report within **48 hours** of delivery through the The Platform Returns Center
  3. The Platform will issue a full refund or send a replacement (customer choice)
- The customer is not required to return the damaged item.

### 6.4 Delivery Attempts
- Carriers make up to **3 delivery attempts** for packages requiring a signature.
- After the third failed attempt, the package is returned to the The Platform fulfillment center.
- The customer is notified via email each time an attempt is made and can provide delivery instructions (e.g., leave with neighbor, safe spot).
- Platinum members can set permanent delivery preferences in their account settings.

## 7. Package Tracking

### 7.1 Tracking Notifications
Customers receive automated notifications at the following stages:
1. **Order Confirmed:** Order received and payment processed
2. **Order Shipped:** Package picked up by carrier, tracking number provided
3. **In Transit:** Package en route (daily updates)
4. **Out for Delivery:** Package on the delivery vehicle
5. **Delivered:** Package delivered to the specified address

### 7.2 Tracking Issues
- If tracking information is not available within **48 hours** of order shipment confirmation, customers should contact The Platform support.
- Tracking discrepancies (e.g., shows delivered but customer hasn't received) are handled under Section 6.2 (Lost Packages) if not resolved within 48 hours.
