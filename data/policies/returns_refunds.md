## 1. Overview

The platform is committed to ensuring customer satisfaction with every purchase. This policy outlines the terms, conditions, and procedures for processing returns and refunds across all product categories sold on the platform. All customer support agents must strictly adhere to these guidelines when handling return and refund requests.

## 2. General Return Window

### 2.1 Standard Return Window
- All products purchased from the platform are eligible for return within **30 calendar days** from the date of delivery.
- The return window begins on the day the package is marked as "Delivered" in the platform logistics system, regardless of when the customer physically receives or opens the package.
- Returns initiated after 30 days will be automatically declined unless an exception applies (see Section 6).

### 2.2 Extended Return Window — Holiday Season
- For purchases made between **November 15 and December 31**, the return window is extended to **60 calendar days** from the delivery date.
- This extension applies automatically to all eligible products and requires no additional action from the customer.
- The holiday extension does not apply to perishable goods, digital products, or final sale items.

### 2.3 Extended Return Window — Loyalty Members
- **Gold Tier** members receive a **45-day** return window on all eligible purchases.
- **Platinum Tier** members receive a **60-day** return window on all eligible purchases.
- Loyalty tier return windows do not stack with the holiday extension; the longer window applies.

## 3. Eligible Products for Return

### 3.1 Products Eligible for Full Refund
The following product categories are eligible for a full refund when returned in original, unopened condition:
- Electronics and accessories (phones, tablets, laptops, headphones, chargers)
- Clothing and footwear (all sizes, all brands)
- Home and kitchen appliances (small appliances, cookware, utensils)
- Books and media (physical books, DVDs, Blu-rays)
- Toys and games (board games, action figures, puzzles)
- Sports and outdoor equipment (fitness gear, camping equipment)
- Furniture and home decor (shipped items under 50 lbs)
- Office supplies and stationery

### 3.2 Products Eligible for Partial Refund (Opened/Used)
Products that have been opened or lightly used may be returned for a partial refund:
- **Electronics:** Opened but functional electronics may be returned for a **85% refund** of the purchase price. Original packaging and all accessories must be included.
- **Clothing (worn once, tags removed):** May be returned for a **90% refund** if washed and in wearable condition.
- **Home appliances (used):** May be returned for a **75% refund** if functional and cleaned.

### 3.3 Products NOT Eligible for Return
The following categories are **final sale** and cannot be returned under any circumstances:
- **Perishable goods:** Fresh food, flowers, live plants, frozen items
- **Hygiene products:** Intimate apparel, swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken), personal care items (opened), face masks
- **Customized/personalized items:** Engraved products, custom prints, monogrammed merchandise
- **Digital products:** Software licenses, digital gift cards, downloadable content, subscription activations
- **Hazardous materials:** Flammable liquids, aerosol cans, batteries sold individually
- **Gift cards:** Physical and digital gift cards (can be transferred but not refunded)
- **Clearance/Final Sale items:** Products explicitly marked as "Final Sale" or "No Returns" at the point of purchase
- **Undergarments and hosiery:** For hygiene reasons, regardless of whether the seal has been broken

## 4. Refund Methods and Processing Times

### 4.1 Refund to Original Payment Method
- **Credit/Debit Card:** Refund processed within **5-7 business days** after return is received and inspected. Actual bank posting may take an additional 3-5 business days.
- **PayPal:** Refund processed within **3-5 business days** after return inspection.
- **Platform Wallet:** Refund processed within **24 hours** after return is received (instant for Platinum members).
- **Cash on Delivery (COD):** Refunded via bank transfer within **7-10 business days**. Customer must provide bank account details.
- **Gift Card Payment:** Refunded as a new gift card balance within **24 hours**.

### 4.2 Store Credit Option
- Customers may opt for **Platform Store Credit** instead of a refund to the original payment method.
- Store credit is processed within **24 hours** and includes a **10% bonus** (e.g., a $50 refund = $55 store credit).
- Store credit never expires and can be used across all product categories.
- Store credit is non-transferable and cannot be converted back to cash.

### 4.3 Exchange Option
- Customers may exchange an item for a different size, color, or variant of the same product at no additional shipping cost.
- Exchanges for a different product are treated as a return + new purchase.
- Exchange requests must be initiated within the standard return window.
- Price differences are charged or refunded accordingly.

## 5. Return Shipping

### 5.1 Free Return Shipping
Free return shipping is provided for:
- Items that arrive damaged, defective, or materially different from the listing
- Incorrect items sent by The Platform or fulfillment errors
- All returns by **Platinum Tier** loyalty members (regardless of reason)
- Items over $100 in value (standard return within return window)

### 5.2 Paid Return Shipping
For all other returns:
- A flat-rate return shipping fee of **$5.99** is deducted from the refund amount.
- For items under $15, the return shipping fee is **$3.99**.
- Customers may also use their own shipping method at their expense.

### 5.3 Return Shipping Labels
- Prepaid return labels are generated automatically through the The Platform Returns Center.
- Labels are valid for **14 days** from the date of return initiation.
- If the label expires, the customer must initiate a new return request.
- Return tracking is available via the The Platform tracking portal.

## 6. Exceptions and Special Cases

### 6.1 Damaged or Defective Items
- Products that arrive damaged, defective, or not as described are eligible for a **full refund** plus free return shipping at any time within the first **90 days** of delivery.
- Customers must provide photographic evidence (at least 2 photos) of the damage/defect.
- In some cases, a replacement may be offered instead of a refund, subject to product availability.
- For items under $25 that arrive damaged, The Platform may issue a full refund without requiring the item to be returned ("Keep & Refund" policy).

### 6.2 Missing Items
- If a package is marked as delivered but the customer reports non-receipt within **48 hours**, The Platform will:
  1. Initiate a carrier investigation (takes 3-5 business days)
  2. If unresolved, issue a full refund or replacement
- Claims made after **72 hours** may require additional verification.
- Repeated missing package claims (3+ within 6 months) trigger a review by the Trust & Safety team.

### 6.3 Wrong Item Received
- Customers who receive an incorrect item are eligible for a **full refund** and free return shipping.
- A replacement of the correct item is shipped immediately upon return initiation (no need to wait for return receipt).
- The customer is not required to return the wrong item if its value is under $25.

### 6.4 Price Adjustment (Post-Purchase Price Drop)
- If an item's price drops within **7 days** of purchase, customers may request a price adjustment.
- The difference is refunded to the original payment method.
- Price adjustments are limited to **one per item per customer**.
- Promotional/coupon discounts do not qualify for price adjustment.

## 7. Fraud Prevention in Returns

### 7.1 Return Abuse Detection
The Platform monitors return patterns for potential abuse. The following behaviors may trigger review:
- Excessive return rate (>40% of purchases in any 90-day period)
- Returning items that appear to have been used extensively ("wardrobing")
- Returning different items than what was originally shipped
- Serial return of high-value electronics

### 7.2 Account Restrictions
- Accounts flagged for return abuse may have their return privileges restricted or suspended.
- Affected customers are notified via email and may appeal through the Customer Advocacy team.
- Restrictions are reviewed quarterly.

## 8. Regional Variations

### 8.1 California (USA)
- Per California state law, customers must be informed of the return policy at the point of sale.
- Restocking fees are capped at **15%** of the item's purchase price for electronics.

### 8.2 European Union
- EU customers have a **14-day statutory right of withdrawal** (cooling-off period) for online purchases, starting from the day the goods are received.
- This is independent of and in addition to The Platform's 30-day return window.
- Digital content: Right of withdrawal is waived once downloading/streaming begins, provided the customer was informed and gave explicit consent.

### 8.3 India
- Returns are subject to **Indian Consumer Protection Act, 2019** provisions.
- Certain categories (innerwear, grocery, beauty products that have been opened) are non-returnable per platform policy, aligned with industry standards.
- COD refunds are processed via NEFT/IMPS to the customer's registered bank account within **7-10 business days**.

### 8.4 United Kingdom
- Post-Brexit, UK customers retain a **14-day cooling-off period** for distance sales.
- The consumer must bear the cost of returning goods unless the goods are faulty or not as described.
- The Platform extends free return shipping for UK Platinum members regardless.
