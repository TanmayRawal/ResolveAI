## 1. Overview

This policy covers all international shipping operations for The Platform, applicable to shipments destined outside the United States. It includes customs, duties, delivery timelines, and country-specific restrictions.

## 2. Supported Countries and Regions

### 2.1 Tier 1 Countries (Full Service)
The following countries receive full The Platform international support with expedited options:
- **North America:** Canada, Mexico
- **Europe:** United Kingdom, Germany, France, Netherlands, Italy, Spain, Ireland, Sweden, Belgium, Austria, Switzerland, Denmark, Norway, Finland, Poland, Portugal
- **Asia-Pacific:** Japan, South Korea, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, India
- **Middle East:** United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia

### 2.2 Tier 2 Countries (Standard Service)
Standard international shipping only. No expedited or overnight options:
- All other EU member states not listed in Tier 1
- Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Colombia
- South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt
- Thailand, Malaysia, Philippines, Indonesia, Vietnam
- Israel, Turkey

### 2.3 Restricted/Unsupported Countries
The Platform does not ship to the following due to regulatory or logistical constraints:
- Countries under US sanctions (Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, Crimea region)
- Countries with unreliable postal systems where shipment insurance is unavailable

## 3. International Shipping Methods

### 3.1 International Standard
- **Delivery Time:** 10-21 business days
- **Cost:** Starts at $14.99 (varies by weight, dimensions, and destination)
- **Carriers:** USPS International, DHL eCommerce
- **Tracking:** Available but may be limited within destination country

### 3.2 International Expedited
- **Delivery Time:** 5-10 business days
- **Cost:** Starts at $24.99 (varies by destination)
- **Carriers:** DHL Express, UPS Worldwide Expedited, FedEx International Economy
- **Tracking:** Full door-to-door tracking
- **Availability:** Tier 1 countries only

### 3.3 International Priority
- **Delivery Time:** 2-5 business days
- **Cost:** Starts at $39.99 (varies by destination)
- **Carriers:** DHL Express, FedEx International Priority
- **Tracking:** Full real-time tracking with delivery confirmation
- **Availability:** Tier 1 countries only

## 4. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

### 4.1 General Policy
- All international orders may be subject to **import duties, taxes, and customs fees** imposed by the destination country's government.
- **The Platform does NOT include customs duties or import taxes** in the product price or shipping cost. These are the **customer's responsibility** and are collected separately by the carrier at delivery or by the local customs authority.
- Customers are advised to check their country's customs regulations before placing an order.

### 4.2 Duties Prepaid Option (DDP — Delivered Duty Paid)
- Available for **Tier 1 countries only** at an additional cost (calculated at checkout).
- The Platform pays the duties and taxes upfront so the customer has no surprise charges at delivery.
- DDP pricing is an **estimate** and is non-refundable, even if actual duties are lower.

### 4.3 Customs Declarations
- The Platform accurately declares the value and contents of all international shipments on customs forms.
- Customers must not request undervaluation or false declarations. Such requests will be declined.
- Gift declarations are applied only when items are shipped to a different address from the billing address and the customer selects the "Gift" option at checkout.

### 4.4 Customs Delays
- The Platform is not responsible for delays caused by customs inspection or clearance.
- If a package is held in customs for more than **15 business days**, The Platform will assist the customer in contacting the carrier for resolution.
- Customs rejections (e.g., prohibited items) result in the package being returned to The Platform. The customer is refunded the product price minus shipping costs.

## 5. International Returns

### 5.1 Return Window
- International customers have the same **30-day return window** from delivery (60 days for holiday purchases, extended for loyalty tiers).
- EU customers retain their **14-day statutory cooling-off right** per EU consumer protection law.
- UK customers have a **14-day cooling-off right** per UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.

### 5.2 Return Shipping Costs
- **Customer-paid returns:** International return shipping is the customer's responsibility unless the item is damaged, defective, or incorrectly shipped.
- **The Platform-paid returns:** For damaged/defective items, The Platform provides a prepaid return label for Tier 1 countries. For Tier 2 countries, The Platform reimburses up to $25 in return shipping costs upon receipt of a shipping receipt.
- **Platinum members:** Free return shipping from all Tier 1 and Tier 2 countries.

### 5.3 Customs Duties on Returns
- Customs duties paid by the customer on the original delivery are **NOT refunded by The Platform**, as these are collected by the destination country's government.
- Customers should contact their local customs authority for duty reclaim procedures.

## 6. Country-Specific Rules

### 6.1 Canada
- Shipments under **CAD $20** are generally duty-free.
- GST/HST applies to most imported goods.
- The Platform offers DDP option for Canadian orders.
- Delivery to remote areas (Northern Canada) may take an additional 5-10 business days.

### 6.2 European Union
- Per EU regulations, VAT is charged on all goods imported into the EU.
- For shipments valued under **€150**, The Platform collects VAT at checkout via the EU's Import One-Stop Shop (IOSS) system, eliminating surprise charges at delivery.
- For shipments over €150, customs duties + VAT are collected at delivery.
- All EU returns benefit from the 14-day cooling-off period under the EU Consumer Rights Directive.

### 6.3 United Kingdom
- UK VAT (20%) is collected at checkout for shipments under **£135** through the UK's simplified customs process.
- For shipments over £135, duties and VAT are collected by the carrier at the point of delivery.
- Post-Brexit, UK is treated as a Tier 1 international destination with full shipping options.

### 6.4 India
- Import duties on electronics can range from **10-30%** depending on the product category.
- The Platform provides an estimated duty calculator at checkout for Indian customers.
- Cash on Delivery is not available for international shipments to India.
- Delivery in India is handled by BlueDart / DTDC for the last-mile segment.

### 6.5 Australia
- GST (10%) applies to all goods imported into Australia valued over **AUD $1,000**.
- For goods under AUD $1,000, GST is collected at checkout by The Platform.
- Certain items (e.g., food products, plant-based materials, wooden items) may be subject to inspection by the Australian Border Force.

### 6.6 Japan
- Consumption tax (10%) applies to all imported goods.
- Customs duties vary by product category (typically 0-15% for consumer goods).
- Japan Post handles the final domestic delivery.
- Delivery address must be provided in both English and Japanese characters.

## 7. International Shipping Restrictions

### 7.1 Prohibited Items (International)
In addition to domestic restrictions, the following items cannot be shipped internationally:
- Currency and negotiable instruments
- Precious metals (gold, silver) in bulk
- Weapons of any kind (including replicas)
- Controlled substances and drug paraphernalia
- Counterfeit goods
- Any item prohibited by the destination country's import regulations

### 7.2 Value Limits
- Single package value must not exceed **$2,500** for standard international shipping.
- For items valued over $2,500, only International Priority shipping with enhanced insurance is available.
- Items valued over $10,000 require formal customs entry and additional documentation.
