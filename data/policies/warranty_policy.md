## 1. Overview

This policy outlines the warranty coverage, claims process, and limitations for products sold on The Platform. Warranty coverage varies by product category and seller type.

## 2. The Platform Standard Warranty

### 2.1 Coverage Period
- **Electronics (laptops, tablets, phones, monitors):** 1-year limited warranty from the date of delivery
- **Small appliances (blenders, coffee makers, toasters):** 1-year limited warranty
- **Large appliances (refrigerators, washers, dryers):** 2-year limited warranty
- **Furniture:** 1-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects
- **Clothing and accessories:** 90-day warranty against manufacturing defects (stitching, zippers, fabric tears under normal use)
- **Toys and sports equipment:** 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects

### 2.2 What's Covered
The The Platform Standard Warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects (hardware malfunction, component failure under normal use)
- Materials defects (premature wear, discoloration, or degradation under normal conditions)
- Assembly defects (parts missing from factory, misaligned components)

### 2.3 What's NOT Covered
The warranty does NOT cover:
- **Physical damage:** Drops, spills, cracks, scratches, or any damage resulting from misuse, abuse, or accidents
- **Water damage:** Unless the product is rated as water-resistant/waterproof (e.g., IP67/IP68)
- **Normal wear and tear:** Battery degradation, cosmetic wear, fading
- **Unauthorized modifications:** Products modified, rooted, jailbroken, or repaired by unauthorized service providers
- **Software issues:** Operating system bugs, third-party app compatibility, or malware (for electronics)
- **Consumable components:** Battery replacements beyond 6 months, printer ink/toner, filters, bulbs
- **Cosmetic imperfections:** Minor scratches or blemishes that do not affect functionality
- **Acts of God:** Damage caused by natural disasters, power surges (unless a surge protector was used and failed), or extreme environmental conditions

## 3. Extended Warranty (The Platform Protection Plan)

### 3.1 Plan Options
- **1-Year Extension:** Extends coverage by 12 months after the standard warranty expires. Cost: 8-12% of product price.
- **2-Year Extension:** Extends coverage by 24 months. Cost: 14-18% of product price.
- **Accidental Damage Protection (ADP):** Covers drops, spills, and cracks. Can be added to either extension plan for an additional 5% of product price.

### 3.2 Extended Warranty Coverage
In addition to standard warranty coverage, the extended warranty includes:
- **Power surge protection:** Coverage for damage caused by power surges
- **Mechanical/electrical failure:** Extended coverage for component failures beyond the standard period
- **No deductibles:** All covered claims are processed without deductibles
- **Replacement guarantee:** If the product cannot be repaired, a replacement of equal or greater value is provided

### 3.3 How to Purchase
- Extended warranty plans can be purchased at the time of product purchase (at checkout) or within **30 days** of delivery
- Plans cannot be purchased after 30 days from delivery
- Plans are non-transferable and tied to the original purchaser's The Platform account
- Plans are non-refundable after 30 days of purchase (full refund available within 30 days if no claims have been filed)

## 4. Warranty Claims Process

### 4.1 How to File a Claim
1. Go to the The Platform Warranty Center (Order History → Select Item → "Warranty Claim")
2. Describe the issue and provide supporting photos/videos
3. The Platform reviews the claim within **3 business days**
4. If approved:
   - For items under $100: A replacement is shipped, and the customer is NOT required to return the defective item
   - For items $100+: A prepaid return label is provided; replacement/refund is issued upon receipt and inspection of the defective item
5. If denied: The customer receives a detailed explanation and may appeal within **14 days**

### 4.2 Repair vs. Replace Decision
- The Platform reserves the right to repair or replace the defective product at its discretion
- If a replacement of the exact model is unavailable, a comparable or upgraded model is offered
- The customer may choose a full refund instead of a replacement (refund based on the current depreciated value for claims outside the first 6 months — see depreciation schedule below)

### 4.3 Depreciation Schedule (for refund-in-lieu-of-replacement)
| Claim Timing | Refund Value |
|--------------|-------------|
| 0-6 months | 100% of purchase price |
| 6-12 months | 85% of purchase price |
| 12-18 months (extended warranty) | 70% of purchase price |
| 18-24 months (extended warranty) | 60% of purchase price |
| 24+ months (extended warranty) | 50% of purchase price |

## 5. Third-Party Seller Warranties

### 5.1 Seller-Provided Warranties
- Third-party sellers may offer their own warranty terms, which are displayed on the product page
- Seller warranties are administered by the seller, not The Platform
- The Platform does not guarantee or take responsibility for seller warranty claims
- If a seller fails to honor their stated warranty, the customer may file an A-to-Z claim (see Marketplace Seller Policy)

### 5.2 Manufacturer Warranties
- Many products sold on The Platform (both 1P and 3P) come with manufacturer warranties
- Manufacturer warranty claims must be filed directly with the manufacturer
- The Platform provides the purchase receipt and proof of authenticity upon customer request to facilitate manufacturer warranty claims
- The Platform is not a party to manufacturer warranty agreements

## 6. Regional Warranty Variations

### 6.1 European Union
- Per EU law, consumers have a minimum **2-year legal guarantee** on all consumer goods, regardless of the seller's stated warranty period
- This legal guarantee covers any defect that becomes apparent within 2 years of delivery
- For defects appearing within the first **12 months**, the defect is presumed to have existed at the time of delivery (burden of proof on the seller)

### 6.2 United Kingdom
- UK consumers are protected by the **Consumer Rights Act 2015**, which provides a 30-day right to reject faulty goods for a full refund, and up to **6 years** to claim for goods that do not conform to the contract

### 6.3 Australia
- Australian Consumer Law provides guarantees that cannot be excluded by warranty terms. Products must be of acceptable quality, fit for purpose, and match their description, regardless of warranty period
