## 1. Overview

This policy describes how The Platform collects, uses, stores, and protects customer personal data. It applies to all users of the The Platform platform, including the website, mobile app, and customer support interactions.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender (optional)
- **Billing information:** Credit/debit card numbers (tokenized), billing address, bank account details (for COD refunds)
- **Shipping information:** Delivery addresses, delivery instructions, recipient names
- **Communications:** Customer support chat transcripts, emails, phone call recordings (with consent), product reviews
- **Preferences:** Language, currency, notification settings, marketing opt-in/out

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
- **Browsing behavior:** Pages viewed, products searched, items added to cart/wishlist, time on page
- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, device type, screen resolution, IP address
- **Location data:** Approximate location based on IP address; precise location only with explicit consent (for same-day delivery)
- **Transaction history:** Purchase history, return history, payment method usage
- **Cookies and tracking:** Session cookies, persistent cookies, third-party analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel)

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### 3.1 Core Business Purposes
- Processing and fulfilling orders
- Communicating about orders (confirmation, shipping, delivery)
- Processing returns, refunds, and warranty claims
- Managing your The Platform account and loyalty program
- Fraud prevention and security monitoring
- Customer support and dispute resolution

### 3.2 Personalization
- Recommending products based on browsing and purchase history
- Customizing the homepage and email content
- Tailoring promotional offers based on preferences and behavior

### 3.3 Communication
- Order status updates (mandatory, cannot be opted out of)
- Marketing emails and promotional offers (opt-in, can be unsubscribed)
- Loyalty program notifications (tier changes, points expiration)
- Survey and feedback requests (opt-in)

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 We Share Data With:
- **Payment processors:** Card networks, PayPal, BNPL providers — for transaction processing
- **Shipping carriers:** Name, address, phone number — for delivery purposes
- **Third-party sellers:** Name, shipping address, order details — for marketplace order fulfillment
- **Analytics providers:** Aggregated, anonymized data for business intelligence (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)
- **Legal/regulatory authorities:** When required by law, court order, or regulatory investigation

### 4.2 We Do NOT:
- Sell personal data to data brokers or advertising networks
- Share full payment card details with sellers or other third parties
- Use customer data for purposes other than those described in this policy without explicit consent

## 5. Customer Data Rights

### 5.1 All Customers
- **Access:** Request a copy of all personal data The Platform holds about you
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate personal data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Opt-out:** Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time

### 5.2 EU/UK Customers (GDPR/UK GDPR)
In addition to the above:
- **Data portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Right to restrict processing:** Request that The Platform limit how your data is used
- **Right to object:** Object to data processing for legitimate interests or direct marketing
- **Right not to be subject to automated decision-making:** Request human review of automated decisions that significantly affect you (e.g., fraud scoring)

### 5.3 California Customers (CCPA/CPRA)
- **Right to know:** Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected
- **Right to delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to opt out of sale/sharing:** The Platform does NOT sell personal data, but you can opt out of data sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising
- **Right to non-discrimination:** The Platform will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account information | Duration of account + 3 years after deletion |
| Transaction history | 7 years (regulatory requirement) |
| Customer support transcripts | 3 years from last interaction |
| Browsing behavior | 2 years from collection |
| Marketing preferences | Duration of account |
| Payment data (tokenized) | Duration of account + removal within 30 days of account closure |

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Technical Measures
- All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- PCI DSS Level 1 compliance for payment data
- Regular penetration testing (quarterly) and vulnerability assessments
- SOC 2 Type II certification
- Data centers located in the US (Oregon, Virginia) with redundancy

### 7.2 Incident Response
- In the event of a data breach:
  1. Contain the breach and assess scope
  2. Notify affected customers within **72 hours** (per GDPR and most US state laws)
  3. Notify relevant regulatory authorities (DPA for EU, AG for US states)
  4. Provide affected customers with free credit monitoring for **12 months**
  5. Publish a public incident report within 30 days

## 8. Customer Support Data Handling

### 8.1 Rules for Support Agents
When handling customer support interactions, agents must:
- **Never ask for full credit card numbers** — only the last 4 digits for verification
- **Never share customer data** with other customers, sellers, or unauthorized parties
- **Always verify identity** before making account changes (2 of 3: account email, phone on file, last order number)
- **Mask sensitive data** in support tickets and notes (no full SSN, no full card numbers)
- **Record calls only with consent** and inform the customer at the start of the call

### 8.2 Data Minimization
- Collect only the data necessary to resolve the support issue
- Do not retain customer data beyond what is captured in the ticket system
- Redact any sensitive data inadvertently shared by the customer in chat/email
