## 1. Overview

This policy outlines the accepted payment methods, billing procedures, payment security measures, and dispute resolution processes for all transactions on the The Platform platform.

## 2. Accepted Payment Methods

### 2.1 Credit and Debit Cards
- **Visa** (credit, debit, prepaid)
- **Mastercard** (credit, debit, prepaid)
- **American Express** (credit only)
- **Discover** (credit, debit)
- All cards must be 3D Secure enabled for transactions over $500.

### 2.2 Digital Wallets
- **PayPal** (including PayPal Credit and Pay in 4)
- **Apple Pay** (on iOS devices and Safari browser)
- **Google Pay** (on Android devices and Chrome browser)
- **The Platform Wallet** (preloaded balance, store credit, or gift card balance)
- **Amazon Pay** (via integrated checkout)

### 2.3 Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)
- **Affirm:** Available for purchases between $50 and $5,000. 0% APR for select items (promotional periods), otherwise standard APR applies.
- **Klarna:** Pay in 4 installments (interest-free) for purchases up to $1,000. Monthly financing for large purchases.
- **Afterpay:** 4 interest-free installments for purchases between $35 and $2,000.
- BNPL is not available for gift cards, digital products, or subscription orders.

### 2.4 Cash on Delivery (COD)
- Available for domestic orders within the US (select regions) and India.
- COD orders are limited to a maximum value of **$200 (US)** or **₹15,000 (India)**.
- A flat COD handling fee of **$2.99 (US)** or **₹49 (India)** applies.
- COD is not available for marketplace/third-party seller items or pre-orders.

### 2.5 Gift Cards and Store Credit
- The Platform gift cards (physical and digital) can be used as full or partial payment.
- Multiple gift cards can be combined on a single order.
- Store credit earned through returns, promotions, or loyalty rewards is automatically applied to the The Platform Wallet.
- Gift cards cannot be used to purchase other gift cards.

### 2.6 Cryptocurrency (Beta)
- The Platform accepts **Bitcoin (BTC)** and **Ethereum (ETH)** via the Coinbase Commerce integration.
- Available for select product categories only. Not available for subscription orders.
- Cryptocurrency payments are final and non-refundable. Refunds for crypto payments are issued as The Platform Store Credit.
- This is a beta feature and may be discontinued without notice.

## 3. Billing and Authorization

### 3.1 Authorization Holds
- When an order is placed, The Platform places an **authorization hold** on the payment method for the full order amount.
- The hold is not a charge — it temporarily reserves funds to ensure they are available when the order ships.
- Authorization holds are released automatically if the order is cancelled or after **7 days** if the order has not shipped.
- Some banks may take up to **10 business days** to release expired authorization holds.

### 3.2 Charge Timing
- **Credit/Debit Cards:** Charged when the order ships (not when placed).
- **PayPal:** Charged immediately upon order confirmation.
- **Digital Wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay):** Charged when the order ships.
- **BNPL:** First installment charged immediately; subsequent installments per the BNPL provider's schedule.
- **Gift Cards/Store Credit:** Deducted immediately upon order confirmation.
- **COD:** Collected at the time of delivery by the carrier.
- **Cryptocurrency:** Charged immediately upon order confirmation (blockchain confirmation required).

### 3.3 Multi-Item Order Billing
- For multi-item orders, The Platform may process separate charges if items ship from different fulfillment centers or at different times.
- Each charge corresponds to a shipment and includes only the items in that specific shipment.
- The customer's bank statement may show multiple charges that, combined, equal the original order total.

## 4. Payment Security

### 4.1 Fraud Detection
- All transactions are screened through The Platform's fraud detection system, which evaluates:
  - Billing and shipping address match
  - Transaction amount vs. historical spending patterns
  - Device fingerprint and IP geolocation
  - Velocity checks (multiple orders in a short period)
- Orders flagged as potentially fraudulent are placed on hold for **manual review** (up to 24 hours).
- The customer may be asked to verify their identity through a secondary verification step (SMS code, email verification, or ID upload).

### 4.2 Secure Transactions
- All payment data is encrypted using **TLS 1.3** during transmission.
- The Platform is **PCI DSS Level 1** compliant. Card data is never stored on The Platform servers; it is tokenized through our payment processor.
- Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) is available and recommended for account security.

### 4.3 Declined Payments
- If a payment is declined, the customer is notified immediately and given the opportunity to:
  1. Retry with the same payment method
  2. Use an alternative payment method
  3. Contact their bank/card issuer
- Orders with declined payments are held for **24 hours** before automatic cancellation.
- If the customer's card is repeatedly declined (3+ attempts), the order is cancelled, and the customer is asked to contact their bank.

## 5. Payment Disputes and Chargebacks

### 5.1 Customer-Initiated Disputes
- Customers should first contact The Platform support before filing a payment dispute with their bank/card issuer.
- The Platform resolves most disputes within **48 hours** of initial contact.
- If a dispute cannot be resolved through support, the customer may escalate to the Payment Disputes team.

### 5.2 Chargebacks
- If a customer files a chargeback with their bank without first contacting The Platform:
  - The Platform will attempt to resolve the issue and may provide evidence to the card issuer to contest the chargeback.
  - Orders associated with successful chargebacks are treated as returns, and any future orders from the account may be subject to additional verification.
- Accounts with **3 or more chargebacks** in a 12-month period are subject to suspension and review by the Trust & Safety team.

### 5.3 Unauthorized Charges
- If a customer identifies an unauthorized charge from The Platform:
  1. Contact The Platform support immediately
  2. The account is frozen pending investigation
  3. Unauthorized transactions are fully refunded within **3 business days** of confirmation
  4. The customer is advised to update their password and enable 2FA

## 6. Pricing and Tax

### 6.1 Price Display
- All prices on The Platform are displayed exclusive of tax (pre-tax) for US customers.
- International prices may or may not include VAT/GST depending on the destination (see International Shipping Policy).

### 6.2 Sales Tax
- The Platform collects applicable state and local sales tax based on the shipping destination.
- The Platform is registered for sales tax collection in all 50 US states and DC where applicable.
- Tax-exempt customers (e.g., qualifying organizations) may apply for tax-exempt status by uploading a valid exemption certificate. Processing takes 3-5 business days.

### 6.3 Currency
- All domestic US transactions are in USD.
- International customers see prices in their local currency (converted at checkout using live exchange rates).
- The actual charge amount may vary slightly from the displayed amount due to exchange rate fluctuations between checkout and payment processing.
