## 1. Overview

The Platform operates as both a direct retailer and a marketplace platform. This policy governs the relationship between The Platform, third-party sellers, and customers for marketplace transactions. It outlines seller obligations, customer protections, and dispute resolution for marketplace orders.

## 2. Marketplace Structure

### 2.1 Seller Types
- **The Platform Direct (1P):** Products sold and fulfilled by The Platform. Full The Platform policies apply.
- **Fulfilled by The Platform (FBS):** Products sold by third-party sellers but stored, packed, and shipped by The Platform fulfillment centers. The Platform return/shipping policies apply.
- **Seller-Fulfilled (3P):** Products sold and shipped directly by the third-party seller. Seller's own return/refund policies apply, subject to The Platform minimum standards (see Section 3).

### 2.2 Identifying Seller Type
- The product page clearly identifies the seller: "Sold by [Seller Name]" and "Fulfilled by [The Platform/Seller Name]."
- Customers can filter search results by fulfillment type.
- The order confirmation email specifies the seller and fulfillment method for each item.

## 3. Minimum Standards for Seller-Fulfilled (3P) Orders

### 3.1 Return Policy Minimums
Third-party sellers on The Platform must adhere to the following minimum return standards:
- **Return window:** At least **15 days** from delivery (vs. The Platform's 30-day standard)
- **Refund processing:** Within **7 business days** of receiving the returned item
- **Damaged/defective items:** Full refund + free return shipping (mandatory, non-negotiable)
- **Wrong item received:** Full refund + free return shipping + immediate correct item shipment (if available)
- Sellers may offer more generous policies but cannot fall below these minimums.

### 3.2 Shipping Standards
- Sellers must ship orders within **3 business days** of order confirmation (or the listed handling time, whichever is longer)
- All shipments must include tracking information uploaded to the The Platform platform within **24 hours** of shipment
- Sellers must achieve a minimum on-time delivery rate of **90%**
- Sellers falling below shipping performance standards receive warnings; repeated violations result in listing suspension

### 3.3 Communication Standards
- Sellers must respond to customer messages within **24 hours** (business days)
- Sellers must provide clear, accurate product listings (description, dimensions, materials, images)
- Misleading or inaccurate listings result in listing removal and potential seller account suspension

## 4. Customer Protections for Marketplace Orders

### 4.1 The Platform A-to-Z Purchase Protection
All marketplace purchases are covered by the The Platform A-to-Z Purchase Protection program:
- **Coverage:** If a seller fails to deliver the item, sends the wrong item, sends a materially different item, or refuses a valid return — the customer can file an A-to-Z claim.
- **Filing window:** Within **30 days** of the latest estimated delivery date, or within **30 days** of the return request (if return-related).
- **Process:**
  1. Customer contacts the seller first and waits **48 hours** for a response.
  2. If unresolved, the customer files an A-to-Z claim through the Resolution Center.
  3. The Platform reviews the claim within **5 business days**.
  4. If the claim is approved, The Platform issues a **full refund** from its own funds and recovers the amount from the seller.

### 4.2 Claim Eligibility
A-to-Z claims are eligible when:
- Item not received and tracking shows no delivery
- Item received is materially different from the listing (wrong product, wrong size, damaged beyond use)
- Seller refuses a return that falls within the seller's stated return policy
- Seller does not respond to the customer within 48 hours of a return/issue request

A-to-Z claims are NOT eligible when:
- Customer has already received a refund from the seller
- The dispute is about personal taste or subjective quality (not a defect or mismatch)
- The item was damaged after delivery due to customer handling
- The customer filed a chargeback with their bank (cannot dual-claim)

### 4.3 Seller-Fulfilled Returns Process
1. Customer initiates a return through the The Platform Returns Center
2. The return request is sent to the seller with a **48-hour response deadline**
3. Seller provides a return shipping label or instructions
4. Customer ships the item back using the provided method
5. Seller inspects and processes the refund
6. If the seller does not respond or refuses without valid reason, The Platform intervenes (see A-to-Z protection)

## 5. Marketplace Disputes

### 5.1 Buyer-Seller Communication
- All communications between buyers and sellers must go through the The Platform messaging system for record-keeping.
- The Platform may review message history when resolving disputes.
- Harassment, threats, or abusive language in messages results in immediate action against the offending party.

### 5.2 Dispute Resolution Timeline
| Step | Action | Deadline |
|------|--------|----------|
| 1 | Customer contacts seller | — |
| 2 | Seller responds | 48 hours |
| 3 | If unresolved, customer files A-to-Z claim | Within 30 days |
| 4 | The Platform reviews claim | 5 business days |
| 5 | Decision issued; refund or denial with explanation | Same day as review |
| 6 | Appeal (if denied) | Within 7 days |
| 7 | Final appeal decision | 5 business days |

### 5.3 Seller Impact of Claims
- Sellers with an A-to-Z claim rate exceeding **1%** of total orders receive a warning
- Sellers with a claim rate exceeding **2%** have listing visibility reduced
- Sellers with a claim rate exceeding **5%** are subject to account suspension and review
- Claim rate is calculated on a rolling 90-day basis

## 6. Counterfeit and Prohibited Items

### 6.1 Anti-Counterfeit Policy
- Selling counterfeit, replica, or knockoff items on The Platform is strictly prohibited
- A single verified counterfeit complaint results in **immediate listing removal** and investigation
- Confirmed counterfeit sellers face **permanent account termination** and funds withholding
- Customers who receive suspected counterfeit items should report through the "Report Counterfeit" button on the order page

### 6.2 Prohibited Marketplace Items
- Recalled products
- Expired consumables (food, supplements, cosmetics past expiration date)
- Items that infringe intellectual property rights
- Regulated items without proper documentation (e.g., FDA-regulated supplements without labeling compliance)
