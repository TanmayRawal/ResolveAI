## 1. Overview

This policy outlines the protections and rights afforded to buyers purchasing from third-party sellers on the The Platform Marketplace. It complements the Returns & Refunds Policy and the Marketplace Seller Policy.

## 2. Buyer Rights

### 2.1 Right to Accurate Product Information
- Buyers have the right to receive products that match the seller's listing, including:
  - Product description, specifications, and dimensions
  - Condition (new, refurbished, used — as stated)
  - Images (must be representative; no stock photos that misrepresent the actual product)
  - Country of origin and material composition
- If a product does not match the listing, the buyer is entitled to a full refund + return shipping at the seller's expense.

### 2.2 Right to Timely Delivery
- Buyers have the right to receive their order within the delivery timeline stated on the product page at the time of purchase.
- If the delivery is late by more than **5 business days** beyond the estimated delivery date:
  - The buyer may cancel the order for a full refund.
  - The buyer may choose to wait, in which case the seller must provide a revised delivery estimate.

### 2.3 Right to Communication
- Buyers have the right to a response from the seller within **48 hours** of contacting them through the The Platform messaging platform.
- If the seller fails to respond, the buyer may escalate directly to The Platform without waiting further.

### 2.4 Right to Return
- Buyers have the right to return marketplace items per the seller's stated return policy (which must meet The Platform's minimum standards of a 15-day return window).
- For items that arrive damaged, defective, or materially different from the listing, the buyer has the right to a full refund regardless of the seller's return policy.

## 3. Buyer Responsibilities

### 3.1 Accurate Shipping Information
- Buyers must provide accurate and complete shipping addresses.
- Failed deliveries due to incorrect addresses are the buyer's responsibility, and reshipping costs apply.

### 3.2 Timely Reporting
- Issues with orders (damaged, missing, wrong item) must be reported within **5 business days** of delivery.
- Late claims may be handled at The Platform's discretion but are not guaranteed coverage under A-to-Z protection.

### 3.3 Good Faith Participation
- Buyers must act in good faith when filing claims and return requests.
- Fraudulent claims (returning different items, claiming non-receipt when delivery is confirmed with photo) result in account penalties.
- Buyers with excessive claim rates (>20% of marketplace purchases in any 6-month period) may be flagged for review.

## 4. Refund Guarantees for Marketplace Purchases

### 4.1 Guaranteed Full Refund Scenarios
The buyer receives a **full refund** (product price + original shipping) in these situations:
- Item not received (confirmed non-delivery or no tracking update for 7+ days)
- Item materially different from listing
- Item arrived damaged or defective
- Seller failed to respond to return request within 48 hours
- Seller refused a valid return within their stated policy
- Counterfeit item received (verified by The Platform)

### 4.2 Partial Refund Scenarios
A **partial refund** may be issued when:
- Buyer returns an item outside the seller's return window but within 30 days of delivery (50-80% refund at The Platform's discretion)
- Item shows signs of use beyond what is necessary to inspect it (per seller's assessment, validated by The Platform)
- Buyer and seller agree to a partial refund as a resolution without full return

### 4.3 No Refund Scenarios
A refund is **not issued** when:
- The buyer failed to report the issue within the reporting window and no evidence supports the claim
- The product matches the listing and functions as described (buyer's remorse on a 3P no-return item)
- The buyer has already received a chargeback from their bank for the same transaction

## 5. Marketplace-Specific Escalation Path

1. **Step 1:** Buyer contacts the seller through The Platform messaging
2. **Step 2:** If seller doesn't respond in 48 hours or resolution is unsatisfactory → Buyer files an A-to-Z claim
3. **Step 3:** The Platform Marketplace Support reviews the claim (5 business days)
4. **Step 4:** Decision issued — refund, partial refund, or claim denied (with reason)
5. **Step 5:** Either party may appeal within 7 days; final decision within 5 business days
6. **Step 6:** If the buyer is still dissatisfied after appeal → External arbitration through The Platform's dispute resolution partner (optional, binding)
